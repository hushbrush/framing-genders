## How Cats & Dogs Live in Art/Design Museums?
This project explores keywords “Cat” and “Dog” from art & design category of Smithsonian museums. It includes the Smithsonian American Art Museum, National Museum of African Art, National Portrait Gallery, Cooper Hewitt Smithsonian Design Museum, Freer Gallery of Art and Arthur M. Sackler Gallery, and Hirshhorn Museum and Sculpture Garden, resulting in a total of 1,166 records and group by object type.
