The Greeting Cards

Most of us have received or sent a greeting card, keeping some as treasured mementos. But have you ever wondered about the greeting cards collected by design museum, like the Cooper Hewitt, Smithsonian? This project explores their collection from 577 greeting cards, uncovering the design elements that make these cards memorable.
